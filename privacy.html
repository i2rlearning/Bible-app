1. Introduction

Branchof Israel (the "Company," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and our fundamental commitment to **never share, sell, or rent** your personal information with any third parties, except as required by law.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information primarily to operate our service and communicate with you. The personal information we collect depends on how you interact with us, but generally includes:

Information You Provide Voluntarily:

  • Contact Data: Your name, email address, and any other information you provide when you sign up for a newsletter, create an account, or contact us directly.
  • Communications: Records of any correspondence with us (e.g., customer support emails).

Information Collected Automatically (Non-Personal):

  • Usage Data: Information about how you access and use our service, such as your IP address, browser type, pages viewed, and the time and date of your visit. This data is collected for internal analytics and service improvement and is **not linked to your personal identifying information**.

3. Our Commitment: No Sharing of Personal Information

We are fundamentally committed to protecting your privacy.

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your Personal Data with third parties for their marketing or promotional purposes. Your name, email address, and other identifying information are kept strictly confidential.

Exceptions (Required Disclosures):

We will only disclose your information in the following extremely limited circumstances:

  • With Your Explicit Consent: If you give us specific, informed, and unambiguous permission to share certain information for a specific, one-time purpose.
  • For Legal Compliance: If we are legally required to do so by court order, subpoena, or applicable law, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies (like pixels and web beacons) to track activity on our service and hold certain information.

  • What are Cookies? Cookies are small text files stored on your device that often include an anonymous unique identifier. They are primarily used to ensure the basic functionality of the website and to track usage.
  • Types of Cookies Used:
    • Necessary Cookies: These cookies are essential for the operation of the website (e.g., security, user login, basic site functionality).
    • Analytics/Performance Cookies: We may use these cookies to analyze how our website is used and how it performs. This data is collected in an aggregated and anonymized form (e.g., using Google Analytics) to help us improve the site's functionality.
  • Your Choices Regarding Cookies: You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. However, if you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of our service.
